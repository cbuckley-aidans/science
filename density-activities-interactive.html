Science -- Density

Practice Activities: Finding Density

Drag the coloured tiles into the empty boxes, then press Check Answers

Formula Density = Mass ÷ Volume  |  Units: g/cm³
Activity 01

Wooden Block

Find: Density
Mass = 48 g

Look at the labeled dimensions.
Use them to work out the volume in Step 1.

Step 1

Work out the volume.
V = L × W × H

V = 5 × 4 × 3 =
12 cm³ 35 cm³ 60 cm³ 20 cm³

Step 2

Write the density formula.

Density =
÷
mass volume

Step 3

Calculate the density. Include the units.

Density = 48 ÷ 60 =
1.25 g/cm³ 0.8 g/cm³ 2.9 g/cm³ 12 g/cm³
